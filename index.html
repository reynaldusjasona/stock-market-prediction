About the Platform

Smarter Investing
Through AI

Our platform combines machine learning algorithms with real-time market data to deliver accurate stock price predictions and personalized investment recommendations—helping retail investors make informed, confident decisions.

Real-Time Data Processing

Live market feeds integrated directly into prediction models for up-to-the-second accuracy.

Advanced Technical Analysis

MA, EMA, RSI, MACD, and Bollinger Bands surfaced clearly on interactive charting tools.

Personalized Recommendations

Tailored stock suggestions based on risk profile, portfolio, and market sentiment signals.

Platform Capabilities

Core Features

Everything you need to analyze markets, predict price movements, and make smarter investment decisions — all in one platform.

User Registration & Login

Secure authentication with JWT tokens. Manage your portfolio, watchlists, and personalized alert preferences across sessions.

Auth

Real-Time Stock Data

Live market feeds for prices, volume, bid/ask spreads, and market cap — refreshed in real time for accurate, up-to-date analysis.

Live Data

AI Price Prediction Engine

LSTM-based deep learning models trained on historical price data and technical indicators to forecast short-term stock price movements.

AI / ML

Interactive Charts & Visualization

Candlestick, line, and bar charts with zoom, pan, and overlay support. Visualize historical data alongside AI-predicted price paths.

Charts

Technical Indicators

Full suite: Moving Averages (MA/EMA), Relative Strength Index (RSI), MACD divergence signals, and Bollinger Bands overlaid on charts.

Indicators

Simplified Order Book

Visualise bid/ask depth and market microstructure. Understand supply-demand pressure zones to support entry and exit timing.

Market Depth

Personalized Recommendations

AI-curated stock picks aligned to your risk appetite and portfolio goals. Recommendations update dynamically as market conditions shift.

AI Picks

Alert & Event Detection

Custom price alerts, volume spike notifications, and AI-detected market events such as breakouts, reversals, and earnings surprises.

Alerts

News & Sentiment Analysis

Real-time financial news aggregation with NLP-powered sentiment scoring. Understand how news flow impacts short-term price movements.

NLP / Sentiment

Top Gainers & Losers

At-a-glance leaderboard of today's best and worst performers. Advanced stock screener with filters for sector, volume, P/E ratio, and momentum.

Screener
